Ingredients

  • 5 lbs of live littleneck clams (costco package is perfect size)
  • 8 cloves of garlic minced
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1 bunch Italian flat leaf parsley
  • Italian chile flakes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
