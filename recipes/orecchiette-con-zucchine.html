Ingredients

  • 1 lb De Cecco Orecchiette (1 box)
  • 5-6 zucchini, cut into half moons
  • 8 cloves of garlic, finely minced
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 bunch of fresh herbs, minced (Parsley, basil, etc.)
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • EVOO
  • Aleppo pepper flakes or Italian chile flakes
  • Parmigiano reggiano
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper

Directions

  1. Prep zucchini and cook down in dutch oven until falling apart but not completely mush, add salt to season and aid breakdown. Add garlic, chile flakes, and pepper towards the end of the cooking. Add white wine to deglaze and reduce while cooking pasta.
  2. When pasta is al dente, add to dutch oven and stir to combine with and absorb sauce. Adjust consistency by adding pasta water if sauce reduces too much.
